Join Our Community

Become a member of the San Antonio Philatelic Association and connect with fellow stamp collectors in a friendly, welcoming environment.

Why Join SAPA?

Community

Connect with collectors of all ages and experience levels who share your passion for stamps and postal history.

Knowledge

Learn from experienced collectors through presentations, articles, and informal discussions at our meetings.

Exchange

Trade, buy, and sell stamps with fellow members to enhance your collection in a trusted environment.

Resources

Receive our quarterly PHILATEX newsletter and gain access to club resources, library, and specialized knowledge.

Our Collecting Philosophy

At SAPA, we believe that philately is primarily a hobby to be enjoyed rather than merely an investment. We encourage our members to collect what brings them personal joy, without strict rules or requirements.

Our club embraces the diversity of collecting styles and interests. Whether you focus on a specific country, time period, or theme, you'll find a place in our community. We value freedom of expression within the hobby and share a common goal of increasing our knowledge and appreciation for stamps and postal history.

"The true value of stamp collecting lies not in the monetary worth of your collection, but in the joy, knowledge, and friendships it brings."

Membership Benefits

What You'll Receive

  • Monthly meetings with educational programs
  • Quarterly PHILATEX newsletter (print & digital)
  • Access to club auctions and sales
  • Free admission to our annual exhibition
  • Networking with fellow collectors
  • Opportunity to exhibit at club shows
  • Access to club library resources
  • Discounts on club merchandise

Special Opportunities

Club Competitions

Participate in our annual exhibition with judged displays and friendly competitions.

Members-Only Auctions

Buy and sell at our quarterly auctions featuring quality material from member collections.

Mentorship Program

New collectors can be paired with experienced members for guidance and advice.

Leadership Opportunities

Share your skills by volunteering for club committees or officer positions.

Membership Categories

Regular Membership

$30 /year
  • For individual collectors
  • Full voting privileges
  • All standard membership benefits
  • PHILATEX newsletter subscription
  • Eligibility for club officer positions

Family Membership

$40 /year
  • For households at the same address
  • Includes spouse and children under 18
  • Two voting members per family
  • One copy of the PHILATEX newsletter
  • All family members welcome at meetings

Youth Membership

$15 /year
  • For collectors under 18 years old
  • Digital PHILATEX newsletter
  • Special youth activities and programs
  • Starter stamp packets for new collectors
  • Mentorship opportunities

Life Membership

$500 one-time
  • Lifetime membership with no annual dues
  • Full voting privileges
  • All standard membership benefits
  • Recognition in the PHILATEX newsletter
  • Special name badge indicating Life Member status

Special Circumstances

SAPA is committed to making membership accessible to all collectors. If you face financial hardship or have special circumstances, please contact our Membership Chair to discuss options. We don't want financial constraints to prevent anyone from enjoying our hobby.
