Directions

The Lions Field Adult Center is located on Broadway, just north of downtown San Antonio, near the Witte Museum and Brackenridge Park.

By Car

Parking is available in the center's lot. Additional street parking can be found on nearby residential streets.

Public Transportation

VIA Metropolitan Transit routes 9, 10, and 14 stop within walking distance of the center.

Nearby Landmarks

The center is across from Mahncke Park and close to the San Antonio Botanical Garden.

Absolutely! Visitors are always welcome to attend our monthly meetings. It's a great way to get a feel for the club and meet our members before deciding to join. There's no obligation to become a member after attending.

Our annual membership dues are:

  • Regular Membership: $30 per year
  • Family Membership: $40 per year
  • Youth Membership (under 18): $15 per year
  • Life Membership: $500 one-time payment

For more details on membership benefits, please visit our Membership page.

Yes! We love introducing new collectors to the hobby. We have several resources for beginners:

  • Beginner presentations at monthly meetings
  • Starter packets for new collectors
  • Mentorship program pairing new collectors with experienced members
  • Basic collecting guides in our newsletter

We encourage you to attend a meeting or contact our Membership Chair for more information.

Yes, we do accept stamp donations, particularly for our youth program. We also periodically hold club auctions where members can sell items.

For large collections or valuable items, please contact our President or Treasurer to discuss the best approach. We can help evaluate your collection and suggest appropriate options for selling or donating.

While we don't provide formal appraisals or authentications, our experienced members can often offer informal opinions and guidance on your stamps.

For official appraisals or expertizing services, we recommend the American Philatelic Society's expertizing service or other professional appraisers. We can help direct you to these resources.
