Directions

MacArthur Park Lutheran Church is located on Nacogdoches Road between Loop 410 and Wurzbach Parkway. Our meetings are held in Building 1, which faces Nacogdoches Road.

By Car

Parking is available in the church's parking lot. Look for Building 1 facing Nacogdoches Road.

Public Transportation

VIA Metropolitan Transit has several routes that service the Nacogdoches Road area.

Meeting Schedule

First Friday: BOG Meeting (7:15 PM) & Show & Tell (7:30 PM)
Second Friday: Auction (items by 7:15 PM, starts 7:30 PM)
Other Fridays: Bourse (6:30-8:00 PM) or Programs (7:30 PM)
