Regular Meetings

What to Expect at Our Meetings

SAPA holds monthly meetings on the third Friday of each month at 7:00 PM at the Lions Field Adult Center in San Antonio. Our meetings typically follow this format:

Club Business (15 min)

Brief announcements, treasurer's report, and club business matters.

Program (45 min)

Educational presentation, guest speaker, or club activity focused on philately.

Break (15 min)

Refreshments and informal socializing with fellow collectors.

Trading Session (45 min)

Time for members to buy, sell, and trade stamps and philatelic materials.

For First-Time Visitors

New to SAPA? Here's what to know:

  • Visitors are always welcome! You don't need to be a member to attend.
  • Please arrive a few minutes early to introduce yourself and get settled.
  • Feel free to bring some stamps or covers you're currently interested in.
  • Don't worry about your experience level - we welcome collectors of all backgrounds.
  • We're a friendly group eager to share our knowledge and passion for the hobby.

Have questions before attending? Contact us and we'll be happy to help!
